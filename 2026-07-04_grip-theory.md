# grip theory notes

sun cube ultimate

have grips on center, edges, vertexes (tho these aren't really geometrical, this is really just names).

edge grips are never twistable.

this is like minimal grip sets, bc there isn't only one that works.

underscores separate unordered sets.

[x] mean the piece has the grip.
[~] mean the piece does not have the grip.
[ ] mean the piece may or may not have the grip (indeterminate).

`U_F * Uh' = U_RF`
`U_F * Fh = RUF`

`RUF * Uh = F_U`
`RUF * Fh' = U_F`
